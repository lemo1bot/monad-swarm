**Step 1: Open Screenshot Toolbar**
- Press: **Shift + Command + 5**
- This opens the screen recording controls

**Step 2: Select Recording Type**
- Click "Record Entire Screen" OR "Record Selected Portion"
- (I recommend "Record Entire Screen")

**Step 3: Click "Record"**
- Click the "Record" button
- You'll see a countdown (3, 2, 1...)
